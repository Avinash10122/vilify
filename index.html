Vilify

An Open Source HTML5 Game

View project onGitHub Play Vilify

Vilify is made by the Codecademy HTML5 Game Development Group.

You are a mad scientist,

who has just completed a doomsday device! In 24 hours the device will erupt, destroying the world! Unfortunately, those pesky Super Heroes have discovered your evil plan, and now they are invading your lab! (Why can't they just mind their own business for once! All you are trying to do is only destroying the world!) Now you must build electronic defense towers, mix poisonous potions, and raise the dead! Protect your lab!

How to Play Vilify

Download the project, extract the files and open index.html in a modern web browser. When Vilify is completed it will be put online for all to play.

Want to Help?

First you will need to be added as a collaborator. Just list your GitHub username (or signup for an account) on https://github.com/Logi0/vilify/wiki/Collaborators and you will soon be added.

Asset Creation

At https://github.com/Logi0/vilify/wiki/Asset-Creation there is a list of hero/towers/monsters/etc. that we need images for. Post a link to your artwork there. We recommend uploading your art to http://opengameart.org. Artwork must be licensed under Public Domain or Creative Commons (make sure to put the attribution requirements if licensed under Creative Commons).

Game Design

To help with game design (types of towers, types of monsters, their stats, the story behind the game, etc.) see https://github.com/Logi0/vilify/wiki/Game-Design.
